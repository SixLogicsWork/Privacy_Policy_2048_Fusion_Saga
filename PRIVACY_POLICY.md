# Privacy Policy for 2048 Fusion Saga

**Last Updated: January 9, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to 2048 Fusion Saga ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles information when you use our services.

**2048 Fusion Saga is a fully offline puzzle game that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
**We do not collect any personal information.** Our App does not require:
- Account registration
- Login credentials
- Name, email, or contact details
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes

### Automatically Collected Information
**We do not automatically collect any information.** Our App does not use:
- Analytics services
- Crash reporting tools
- Advertising SDKs
- User behavior tracking

### Local Device Storage
Our App stores the following data **locally on your device only** to provide game functionality:

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage Location |
|-----------|---------|------------------|
| Game Progress | Save your current game state so you can continue playing | Device only |
| High Scores | Track your best scores and achievements | Device only |
| Game Statistics | Display your gameplay statistics (games played, highest tile, etc.) | Device only |
| Settings Preferences | Remember your chosen theme (dark/light mode), sound settings, and grid size | Device only |

**This locally stored data:**
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third parties
- Is not transmitted over the internet
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the App or clearing App data

## Third-Party Services

### Google Fonts
Our App uses Google Fonts to display text. When you first launch the App, font files may be downloaded from Google's servers. This is a standard feature of many mobile applications. Google's privacy policy can be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**No other third-party services are used.**

We do not use:
- Advertising networks
- Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- Social media integrations
- Payment processors
- Cloud storage services

## Children's Privacy

2048 Fusion Saga is a family-friendly puzzle game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or equivalent minimum age in relevant jurisdictions).

Since our App does not collect any personal data, it complies with:
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) requirements for children's data
- Other applicable children's privacy laws

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our App. All game data is stored locally on your device using standard secure storage methods provided by the operating system.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. However, since we do not collect any personal information:

- **Right to Access**: There is no personal data to access
- **Right to Deletion**: You can delete all locally stored game data by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data through your device settings
- **Right to Portability**: Game data is stored locally and not transmitted
- **Right to Opt-Out**: No data collection occurs that requires opt-out

### For European Users (GDPR)
We do not process any personal data as defined under the General Data Protection Regulation. No consent is required as no personal data is collected.

### For California Users (CCPA)
We do not sell personal information. We do not collect personal information as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act.

## Data Retention

We do not retain any user data on our servers because we do not collect any data. Local game data remains on your device until you:
- Uninstall the App
- Clear the App's data through device settings
- Reset your device

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Continued use of the App after any modifications to the Privacy Policy constitutes acceptance of those changes.
